Introducing TeXView

Why should I use TeXView?

Real-time compile

Edit your files either using the web editor or by modifying them with the editor of your choice. TeXView will keep track of the compile things

Collaboration

You can easily share your documents with your friends and colleagues, just send them your edit link

Low on resources

TeXView just needs a TeX environment like texlive and a very basic PHP and NGINX installation to work. You can now use your Raspberry Pi to easily build your TeX files
